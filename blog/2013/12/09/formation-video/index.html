Formation Video

Dec 9th, 2013

Here is a look at my Mobile Payments Hackathon project from back in October, which I call Formation. Jorge, Julian and I built a rails app which uses NokoGiri to web-scrape a google form and append a Stripe payment. The idea is to allow people to add payments to thier Google Forms. Most of this is convered in the video. If you have any questions feel free to reach out.

Cheers, Kyle
